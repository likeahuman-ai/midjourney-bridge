# ToS & Legal

> Full write-up pending v0.1. Summary in [README § Terms of Service](../README.md#️-terms-of-service).

## Key points

- midjourney-bridge replays HTTP calls that the midjourney.com web UI makes — it is a **single-user personal automation tool**, not a proxy, reseller, or pooling service.
- You must use your **own** Midjourney subscription. Sharing, reselling, or pooling accounts is prohibited by Midjourney's ToS and out of scope for this project.
- Automation is a grey area in Midjourney's ToS. Using this at human-level request rates for personal creative work is low-risk; bulk automated generation at scale is not.
- Ban risk is real and lives with you. The maintainers accept no liability.
